Smart Pest Solutions

Smart Pest Solutions is a proudly Basotho-owned company specializing in pest control, weed management, cleaning and disinfection, and the production and supply of ready-to-use chemical solutions.

With extensive local knowledge and a commitment to environmental safety, we provide dependable services to households, businesses, and government institutions across Lesotho. We take pride in an innovative, practical approach that protects health, hygiene, and safety in every environment we serve.

Our Mission

To provide effective, safe, and sustainable pest control and hygiene solutions through quality service, trusted products, and professional expertise, improving the well-being of our clients and communities.

Our Team

We are driven by skilled professionals who are passionate about excellence. From technicians to chemical handlers and customer support personnel, every team member is committed to high-quality service with integrity, attention to detail, and a strong safety culture.

Our Core Values

  1. Integrity: We are honest and ethical in everything we do.
  2. Excellence: We pursue quality, professionalism, and continuous improvement.
  3. Customer Focus: We build lasting relationships by meeting and exceeding expectations.
  4. Safety: We prioritize the health of our clients, team, and environment.
  5. Innovation: We embrace new technologies and methods to deliver better solutions.

What We Do

Pest Control Services

  • Residential and commercial pest control programs
  • Rodent, cockroach, termite, and ant treatment
  • Fly, wasp, gulls and bird control, and mosquito control
  • Seasonal programs: Summer, Fall, Winter, and Spring

Cleaning and Disinfection

  • Post-construction cleaning
  • High-touch surface disinfection, including COVID-19 protocols
  • Deep cleaning and sanitization for schools, clinics, and businesses

Weed Management

  • Herbicide application for lawns, pavements, and open grounds
  • Agricultural and industrial weed treatment solutions
  • Treatment of tree growth, broadleaf, annual, and perennial weeds

Ready-to-Use Chemical Solutions

  • Rodenticides in ready-to-use bait packs
  • Herbicides for broadleaf and grass weeds
  • Indoor and outdoor insecticides
  • Full pit toilet chemicals for waste breakdown and smell reduction
  • Toilet odor-control and freshness-boosting chemical solutions

Service Approach

  1. Before service begins, we evaluate each client's environment and challenge areas.
  2. Our trained inspectors identify infestation sources, breeding areas, and hygiene gaps.
  3. We provide a treatment plan covering applied solutions, safety guidance, and schedules.
  4. We maintain relationships through communication, periodic reviews, and service feedback.
  5. We run seasonal response plans: Summer for insect and rodent control, Fall for prevention and baiting, Winter for indoor monitoring and treatment, and Spring for lawn care, weed control, and pest barrier application.

Client Incentives and Support

  • Free pest control services at the homes of key client personnel during active service contracts.
  • Priority scheduling and a dedicated support line for fast response.
  • On-site awareness and safety training for staff to promote hygiene and pest prevention.
  • Monthly performance reviews to track outcomes and improve service quality.

Health and Safety Standards

  • We avoid indoor liquid chemicals where they may risk health or damage equipment and furniture.
  • We prioritize safer methods such as baiting and fogging to minimize exposure.
  • We use indoor bait stations for controlled, safe, and long-lasting rodenticide application.
  • Our team uses PPE at all times during chemical handling and service delivery.
  • We provide MSDS documentation for chemicals used to ensure transparency and awareness.
  • Treated areas are clearly marked, and clients are guided on safe re-entry times.
  • We follow proper storage, transport, and disposal practices under national regulations.
  • Staff receive regular training on safety protocols and pest control legislation updates.
